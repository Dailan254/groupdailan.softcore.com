About the Faculty

Our History

Established in 1995, the Faculty of Engineering at Softcore University has grown to become one of the leading engineering institutions in the region. We began with just 50 students and have since graduated over 5,000 engineers who are making significant contributions across various industries.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide world-class engineering education through innovative teaching, cutting-edge research, and industry collaboration, preparing graduates to solve complex global challenges.

Vision

To be recognized as a center of excellence in engineering education and research, producing leaders who will drive technological innovation and sustainable development.
